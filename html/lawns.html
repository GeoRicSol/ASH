Lawn Care

Essential UK lawn care involves regular mowing (weekly/fortnightly, March–October).

Key maintenance tasks such as watering, feeding with fertilizer, removing weeds and addressing waterlogged soil.

Seasonal treatments to remove matting (thatch) material.

Key Lawn Care Practices

Mowing: Maintain sharp mower blades to avoid tearing grass. During peak growth, mow once a week, but avoid cutting too low to prevent damage.

Watering: Water deeply every 7–10 days rather than light daily sprinkling to encourage deep roots.

Aeration: Use a garden fork to spike the lawn, which helps air, water, and nutrients reach the roots and alleviates compaction.

Scarification: Remove moss and thatch (dead grass) with a rake to promote healthy growth./p>

Feeding: Use lawn fertilizer to promote strong, thick, green grass.

Repairing: Spread patch repair mix over bare areas, ensuring they are kept moist until established
