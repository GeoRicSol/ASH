Garden Service Testimonials & Reviews

This page will not be used to start with. Only when sufficient feedback has been collected can a honest legitimate page be created. This current page provides notes for the type of information to be collected.

Quality of Work:

♦ How tidy, thorough, and professional the gardeners were ♦ Whether the garden looked noticeably improved ♦ Specific tasks praised (hedge trimming, lawn care, clearance, pruning, etc.)

Reliability & Communication:

♦ Turning up on time ♦ Clear communication before and during the job ♦ Ease of booking and responsiveness

Professionalism & Attitude:

♦ Friendly, respectful, and approachable team ♦ Willingness to go the extra mile ♦ Care taken around property, pets, or delicate plants

Value for Money:

♦ Fair pricing ♦ Transparency with quotes ♦ Whether the client felt the service was worth the cost

Before/After Impact:

♦ How the garden changed ♦ How the client now uses or enjoys the space ♦ Comments like “transformed my garden” or “looks amazing again”

Repeat Business & Recommendations:

♦ Clients saying they would hire again ♦ Clients recommending the service to neighbours or family ♦ Long‑term customers praising consistency

Optional Extras You Can Include:

These help your testimonials feel more authentic and structured: ♦ Client name + area (e.g., “Sarah, Cleethorpes”) ♦ Type of job (e.g., “Garden clearance and hedge reduction”) ♦ Before/after photos ♦ Star rating (if you want a more modern review layout)
