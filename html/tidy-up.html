Misc Garden Task

A well-run garden involves more than just horticulture tasks, a whole ecosystem of non-gardening tasks—things that aren't about plants or soil are essential for the space to function, stay safe, and feel enjoyable.

Repairs & Upkeep:

♦ Fixing or repainting fences, gates, and sheds ♦ Repairing decking boards or loose paving slabs ♦ Maintaining outdoor lighting ♦ Treating wood with preservatives ♦ Repairing garden furniture

General Outdoor Maintenance:

♦ Cleaning patios, decking, and paths ♦ Sweeping leaves from hard surfaces ♦ Power‑washing slippery or mossy areas ♦ Clearing gutters, drains, and soakaways ♦ Removing rubbish, old furniture, or debris

Cleaning & Hygiene:

♦ Cleaning outdoor bins and bin storage areas ♦ Removing algae from walls or steps ♦ Cleaning greenhouses, cold frames, and outdoor storage ♦ Disinfecting pet areas

Safety & Compliance:

♦ Checking for trip hazards (loose slabs, exposed roots, broken steps) ♦ Ensuring handrails and steps are secure ♦ Clearing fire exits or access paths ♦ Removing sharp objects or hazardous waste

DIY & Assembly:

♦ Assembling sheds, pergolas, or garden furniture ♦ Installing or repairing trellises ♦ Putting up hooks, brackets, or storage solutions ♦ Minor painting and decorating outdoors
